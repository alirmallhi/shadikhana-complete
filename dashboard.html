💡 Finding the Right Match
  • 🎯 Look past the checklist. Height, income, and education matter less long-term than shared values, communication style, and how someone treats their family.
  • ✍️ Write a real "Describe Your Ideal Partner." It carries more weight than any other single factor in your match recommendations — a thoughtful answer genuinely brings better matches, not just a nicer profile.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Involve your family early, not just at the end. A trusted second opinion — like the Wali feature on your profile — often catches things you might miss on your own.
  • 🔍 Ask direct questions if something feels vague. A short bio isn't a red flag by itself — but answers that stay vague even after you ask are worth paying attention to.
  • ☕ Take the first meeting seriously before deciding. Messages can be curated — a real conversation tells you more in twenty minutes than a week of texting.
